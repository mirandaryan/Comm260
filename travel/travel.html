Travel to .... Berlin

Located in the north east of the country, Berlin sits on the banks of the river Spree. Geographically the city lies on the European plain, a flat and fertile area of land that extends all the way to Russia.

Perhaps surprisingly for a populous urban area of around 3.7 million people, Berlin is a very green city with over a third of the metropolitan area comprising of gorgeous parks, gardens and forests.

Things to see in Berlin

  • Brandenburg Gate
  • Berlin Wall
  • Checkpoint Charlie
  • Museum Island
